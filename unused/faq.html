General questions:
Is Alef Ba Ta a free platform?
No, Alef Ba Ta platform requires a paid subscription.
Which age group can benefit from Alef Ba Ta?
Alef Ba Ta targets children from 4 to 9 years old, which includes KG and the first years of the elementary school.
Does Alef Ba Ta has an app on the App Store or Google Play?
Alef Ba Ta works from the browser by visiting the website, and there is no app on the App Store or Google Play.
Can I use Alef Ba Ta without an internet connection?
Alef Ba Ta interactive content only works through the platform and requires an internet connection.
Are there any printed books for Alef Ba Ta?
There are no printed books for Alef Ba Ta. However, we provide more than a thousand printable that can be downloaded.
Does Alef Ba Ta support mobiles and/or tablets?
Alef Ba Ta platform only supports desktops, laptops and iPads. Alef Ba Ta doesn't support mobiles and/or Android tablets.
How to contact you?
You can contact us through our Contact Page and we try our best to return to you in the same business day.
Subscription related questions:
Upon subscription, is there any limit on the number of printables that can be downloaded?
No, for subscribed users there is no limit on the number of printables that can be downloaded.
Family subscription related questions:
How can the family benefit from Alef Ba Ta? Is there a defined curriculum for the children to follow?
Alef Ba Ta provides a rich and engaging educational content with thousands of exercises and hundreds of games and stories. However, Alef Ba Ta does not provide a defined curriculum. Therefore, the parents play an important role in selecting the content that best suits their children's level and monitor their performance.
For the family subscription, how many children are allowed to use Alef Ba Ta?
Family subscription allows two children to use the platform. Every child will have his own username and password.
Can more than one device use the same account at the same time?
No. Alef Ba Ta prevents multiple devices from using the same account at the same time.
Study Group & Classroom subscriptions related questions:
What is the difference between a Family subscription and a Study Group or Classroom subscription?
Study Group or Classroom subscription allows the teacher to create accounts for the students, so that every student will have his/her own account. With that, the teacher can create homework, assign it students and monitor their performance. All these features aren't available for a Family subscription.
Is there a subscription for a single teacher without students?
If a teacher wants to use the platform without his students, then he can subscribe as a family. Family subscription allows the teacher to use all the educational content, except for the features related to interacting with the students.
What is the difference between a Study Group and a Classroom subscription?
The difference is in the number of students that can use the platform. For a Study Group, the teacher can create accounts for up to 5 students, while for a Classroom the teacher can can create accounts for up to 25 students.
School subscription related questions:
What are the benefits of using the platform with the students?
When using the platform with the students, the teacher can create and assign homework to the students and monitor their performance. Besides, students can access the platform from their own accounts and work on improving their Arabic skills.
Does each student have his/her own account?
Yes, every student has his/her own username and password.
How can the teacher monitor students performance?
Using the homework feature, which allows the teacher to assess the difficulty of the assigned exercises by viewing the number of mistakes reported and the time took to complete the exercises.
Can the students download the printables?
No, downloading the printables is only available to the teachers.
Are there any tutorials on how to use Alef Ba Ta with the students?
Yes. Upon subscription, the teacher will access a collection of videos that help him/her learn more about the features of the platform and how to use it with the students.
What is the fee for using Alef Ba Ta for a whole school?
We have a fixed pricing policy for schools. For more information, please contact us.
