Sight Words
List of the most common 100 sight words in children books.
Sight words are divided in groups of 5 words, with a review section for each group.
For each sight word, there is an introductory lesson followed by exercises that help understand and memorize the word.
This section helps the child learn and memorize the sight words, which enhance the ability to read simple texts.
There are gifts and certificates to encourage children complete the exercises.
